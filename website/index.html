Why We Built This

Our Mission

Over 466 million people worldwide — and 63 million in India alone — live with disabling hearing loss. Yet 95% of them lack access to the communication tools they need to participate fully in society. SignVoice AI was built to change that.

Who We Serve

Deaf individuals in rural communities, hard-of-hearing students in classrooms, speech-impaired professionals — anyone who has ever felt invisible in a conversation. We build for them first.

Our Vision

A world where language is never a barrier. Where a farmer in rural Bihar can communicate with a doctor. Where a deaf child can learn alongside hearing peers. Where technology amplifies human connection.
